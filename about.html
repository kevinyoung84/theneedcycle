MY STORY

Why I Built The NEED Cycle™

For more than twenty years I've worked in sales, leadership, coaching, customer service, and business development. Although every industry looked different, I kept seeing one common challenge.

People were working incredibly hard to solve problems—but they were often solving the wrong ones.

Sales professionals rushed into presentations before understanding their customers. Leaders addressed symptoms instead of root causes. Organizations invested valuable time, money, and energy into solutions that never solved the real issue.

Eventually I realized these weren't isolated problems. They all stemmed from the same place: people were making decisions before they truly understood the need.

The NEED Cycle™ was created to help change that. It provides a practical framework for asking better questions, building trust, uncovering genuine needs, and making better decisions.

THE PRINCIPLES

What Guides Everything I Do

Understand before you advise.

People deserve to feel heard before they are offered solutions.

Curiosity builds trust.

The strongest relationships begin with thoughtful questions rather than quick answers.

Diagnose before you direct.

Great leaders spend more time understanding the problem than defending a solution.

Solve needs—not symptoms.

Lasting results come from identifying the real problem first.
