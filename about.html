THE STORY

Why The NEED Cycle™ Exists

Every meaningful framework begins with a question.

After more than twenty years in sales, leadership, coaching, customer service and business development, I noticed the same challenge appearing again and again.

People were working incredibly hard—but they were often solving the wrong problems.

That realization became the foundation for The NEED Cycle™, a practical communication framework that helps people slow down, ask better questions, uncover genuine needs, and make better decisions.
