THE FRAMEWORK

What is The NEED Cycle?

A practical framework for understanding what truly drives people.

The NEED Cycle helps professionals move beyond selling, persuading, and surface-level conversations.

By learning to recognize, explore, evaluate, and determine another person's genuine needs, you build trust, create better solutions, and develop stronger relationships.

Whether you're leading a team, coaching a client, growing a business, or strengthening personal relationships, every meaningful conversation begins with understanding the other person's need.
